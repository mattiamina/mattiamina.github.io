About me

Hello, this is Mattia! I am from Savigliano, a small town in the north-west of Italy. I studied in Torino, where I completed my BSc in Physics Engeneering (PoliTO) and my MSc in Nuclear and Subnuclear Physics (UniTO). Currently, I am finishing my PhD in Cosmology and Astrophysics at the Institute of Theoretical Astrophysics (ITA), University of Oslo (UiO).

I am always looking for new challenges and I love to strive for finding the best solution to new problems. I love problem-solving, programming, numerics and computation. I really enjoy working with other people in a team: it means learning more and faster.
