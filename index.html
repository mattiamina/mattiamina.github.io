About me

Hello, this is Mattia! I am from Savigliano, a small town in the north-west of Italy. I studied in Torino, where I completed my B.Sc. in Physics Engineering (PoliTO) and my M.Sc. in Nuclear and Subnuclear Physics (UniTO). Currently, I am finishing my Ph.D. in Cosmology and Astrophysics at the Institute of Theoretical Astrophysics (ITA), University of Oslo (UiO).

I am always looking for new challenges and I like finding the best solution to new problems. I am looking forward to improving my knowledge. I love problem-solving, programming, numerics and computation. I really enjoy working with other people in a team: it means learning more and faster.

Work

As a cosmologist and astrophysicist, my job is to unveil the darkest secrets of the Universe. I try to answer questions like "How did galaxies form?" and "What is dark matter?".

I develop my own algorithms, encoding many different physical phenomena. Starting with a simple picture of the Universe long time ago, I simulate on supercomputers the formation of stars, galaxies and dark matter halos, everything at once!

Comparing the simulation data with astronomical observations, I can decide wether a model works well or if it has to be improved, or even discharged. Among the various models I have been working on, the most interesting ones concern dark matter and its mysterious nature.
