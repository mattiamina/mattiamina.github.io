About me

Hello, this is Mattia! I am from Savigliano, a small town in the north-west of Italy. I studied in Torino, where I completed my BSc in Physics Engeneering (PoliTO) and my MSc in Nuclear and Subnuclear Physics (UniTO). Currently, I am finishing my PhD in Cosmology and Astrophysics at the Institute of Theoretical Astrophysics (ITA), University of Oslo (UiO).

I am always looking for new chellenges and I love to strive for finding the best solution to new problems. I love problem-solving, programming, numerics and computation. I really enjoy working with other people in a team: it means learning more and faster.

Work

As a thoretical cosmologist, my job is try to better understend our own Universe. I mainly worked on the structure formation phase of the Universe, during which matter gravitationally collapses into dark halos, luminous galaxies and clusters of galaxies.

I love computation and, by employing some of the most advanced numerical techniques, I simulate on super computers current models that are challenging our understanding of the Universe, especially those regarding the nature of dark matter and its impact on the formation of structures.
